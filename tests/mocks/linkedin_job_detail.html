About the role

As a Technical Solutions Engineer at Stripe, you will be the technical bridge between our engineering teams and our users. You will help companies integrate Stripe's payment infrastructure, debug complex API issues, and build internal tools to improve our support processes.

What you'll do

  • Investigate and resolve complex technical issues related to Stripe's payment APIs
  • Build internal tools and automation to improve support efficiency
  • Partner with Engineering, Product, and Sales teams on technical escalations
  • Create technical documentation and knowledge base articles
  • Manage cross-functional projects that improve the developer experience

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in a technical support, solutions engineering, or software engineering role
  • Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
  • Experience with REST APIs and payment processing systems
  • Familiarity with async programming (asyncio, event-driven architecture)
  • Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • Excellent communication skills for both technical and non-technical audiences

Nice to have

  • Experience with Docker and Kubernetes
  • Knowledge of FinTech regulations and PCI compliance
  • Prior experience with Salesforce or JIRA for case management
  • Project management certification (PMP, Google PM)
